Life @ Healthians

Robust people practices, a vibrant work culture and an environment that is friendly to learning and individual growth within the organization are what make Healthians a great place to work!
Our employee centric practices ensure the highest standards of fairness, equality, and honesty for all and our vision is to institutionalize a nurturing environment that enables both individual and organizational growth for each person working here.

At Healthians, you will gain exposure to work with skilled professionals and develop your knowledge through focused learning & development initiatives. Healthians actively encourages balancing personal life and professional commitments by providing flexible work schedules and industry leading employee benefit programs.
The fact that we develop our own best practices is one of the single biggest differentiating factors for us. Healthians prides itself on being an equal opportunity employer and its practices are a true reflection of its ethics and ethos.

Fun @ Work

Healthians’s work environment is collegial and abuzz with round-the-year events. We continuously engage our people through an array of activities and events such as Diwali Celebration, New Year’s eve, Valentine’s special and a day with Family @ Healthians. That is not all; you can also enjoy each and every festival in its own special way with Healthians family. From music to games, you will find unlimited fun across our teams.

People Practices

We are committed towards making Healhians a ‘great place to work for’ through our various initiatives.
The following programs will help you understand the ethos of the organization:

Talent Acquisition

At Healthians, recruitment at various levels is done through a competency model which focuses on behavior, skills and credentials. Through this process, Healthians ensures that the selected candidate is the right person not only in terms of his capabilities but also in terms of his alignment with the core values and culture of the organization. That’s not all; our employee referral program called “Prefer to Refer” plays a vital role in our hiring from across the industry and making our work culture even friendlier.

Talent Development

Changing job requirements and fluctuating industry trends make it imperative that companies keep their people up-to-date with the latest skill sets and market information. Healthians continuously endeavors to enhance the capability, creativity and credibility of its people through various learning & development initiatives. While trainings, both internal and external, are on technical and behavioral skills, the prime focus is on grooming employees from within the company and preparing them to take up higher roles at Healthians itself.

Talent Management

We continuously provide career development and growth opportunities to our people through rigorous mentoring and coaching which effectively propels employees to achieve greater heights in performance and enhance their professional and personal growth.

Talent Engagement

By making our work environment open and invigorating, we give our people the opportunity to reinvent themselves and strive for higher grounds. The company encourages job rotation and internal selection at lateral levels to provide multiple career options to its people.

Talent Recognition

Reward and recognition program appreciates and recognizes outstanding performance and achievements by employees through a series of rewards. Not only that; Healthians is known for its strong alumni network, strong cultural foundation and bonding with its employees. That is why a lot of people across lateral positions, including key senior levels, join us again after spending quite a few years outside. A true testament to the strength of Healthians’s people practices and work culture!
