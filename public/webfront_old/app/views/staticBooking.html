Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is your cost so low?

Our cost benefits come from the fact that we have eliminated any intermediaries in the complete process of sample collection to delivering of reports. Additionally, the huge volumes of sample that we provide our labs, give us cost bargaining advantages; the benefits of which are directly passed on to our customers. Hence, we offer all diagnostic packages at the lowest market rate.

2. What happens after I book the order?

After you book a test, you will receive a confirmation call from our team. At the time slot of your choice and convenience our trained phlebotomist will come to your house to collect the blood sample. This sample is then sent to our labs for testing. You will receive an online report (on the email provided by you) within the stipulated time as per the tests. (This time varies as per the tests but is either 24/ 48 hours).

3. How do you ensure quality?

  • 50% of our labs are NABL and the rest of the labs are in the process of getting NABL certification.
  • But we have the best quality process in the country as we stringently follow international quality norms, far above the NABL norms.
  • NABL audits the labs only once a year, whereas we do a weekly 300 check-points audit.
  • We provide daily electronic tracking of every order from the time it is picked up till the time the reports are delivered.
  • NABL audits reports are maintained on paper which labs easily manipulate, whereas we maintain digital records to ensure no manipulation can be done
  • Most NABL certified labs are actually certified only on 3-4 tests but our labs are certified for all test and procedures.
  • We give money back guarantee (on the accuracy of our lab results) as our proof for accuracy and reliability.
  • We are the only labs in India which gives you exact details of where, when and how the blood sample was tested.
  • We are the only labs where temperature control is maintained from the moment the sample is picked till it is tested. Special temperature maintenance packs and      temperature monitoring devices are given to all labs and phlebotomists.

4. What if I don't like your services?

We have served over 1,20,000 customers in NCR alone. We do not just promise accuracy and 100% satisfaction but guarantee it. Yet, if you are not satisfied with our services, we assure 100% money back guarantee.
