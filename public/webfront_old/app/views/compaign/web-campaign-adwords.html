Thyroid

Thyroid function tests are a series of blood tests required in case of hypothyroidism (weight gain, lack of energy, depression, menstrual problems) or hyperthyroidism (weight loss, high levels of anxiety, tremors, cardiac problems, menstrual problems). Available tests include the T3, T3RU, T4, and TSH.

The HbA1c test represents what a person's average blood glucose level was for the last 2 to 3 months before the test. This can help determine how well a person's diabetes is being controlled over time.
